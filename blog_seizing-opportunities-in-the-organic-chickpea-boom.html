Seizing Opportunities in the Organic Chickpea Boom


In the dynamic world of international trade, staying ahead of market trends is required for sustained growth. A recent report by Zion Market Research has unveiled a promising outlook for the organic chickpea market, projecting it to reach a staggering $2.4 billion by 2028, with a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 14.2%.

Chickpeas, also known as garbanzo beans, are nutrient-dense legumes that have been a dietary staple for centuries. What sets organic chickpeas apart is their cultivation without synthetic pesticides and fertilizers, making them a preferred choice for health-conscious consumers. Packed with protein, fibre, and various essential nutrients, organic chickpeas are not only versatile but also contribute to a balanced and wholesome diet.
In 2021-22 chickpea production of 13.75 million tonnes, accounting for nearly 50% of India's total pulse production was done, with major contributing states including Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Uttar Pradesh.
As KGSK Alliance Pvt Ltd, a prominent player in the import and export sector based in India, this news is both exciting and strategically significant. The surge in demand for organic chickpeas presents a golden opportunity to expand our portfolio and tap into this rapidly growing market.
We have always been committed to providing high-quality products to our clients worldwide. The organic food movement has gained immense traction in recent years, with consumers becoming increasingly health-conscious. Organic chickpeas, being a versatile and nutritious ingredient, align perfectly with this trend.
We envision forging new partnerships, facilitating a seamless export supply chain from “source to destination”. Our Collaboration with reliable producers not only guarantees the quality of our products but also contributes to sustainable and ethical business practices.
As we navigate the path to 2028, our focus will be on leveraging our expertise in international trade to capture a significant share of the growing organic chickpea market. By aligning our strategies with consumer preferences and industry trends, KGSK Alliance Pvt Ltd aims to emerge as a key player in this flourishing sector.
