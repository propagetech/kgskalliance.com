International Trade

We are one of the fastest growing international trading companies.

Capitalizing on our strong global network we are able to expand our frontiers from Asian markets, to Australia / New Zealand, Middle East to Africa. KGSK, is ready to build stable food corridors by exporting rice, spices, nuts and frozen vegetables, fruits and Indian snacks worldwide. Committed to be the leader of the trading industry, we source high quality products at competitive prices, and have an efficient delivery system. We also import food products from African market and distribute them all over the world.

Our focus is also to trade in Raw Cashew Nuts (RCN) sourcing it from countries like Benin, Ivory Coast, Ghana, Nigeria and Tanzania. We also trade products like dry split ginger, sesame seeds and Hibiscus flower sourced from African countries.

Exports
