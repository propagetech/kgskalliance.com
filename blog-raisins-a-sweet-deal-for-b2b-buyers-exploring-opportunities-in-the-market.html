Why Raisins?
Raisins are not just dried grapes; they are a powerhouse of nutrients and flavour. Packed with antioxidants, fibre, and natural sweetness, raisins are a popular ingredient in a wide array of products, including baked goods, cereals, snacks, and confectioneries. Their long shelf life and easy portability make them a favourite among manufacturers and retailers alike.
Market Trends and Opportunities
The demand for raisins has been steadily rising, driven by changing consumer preferences towards healthier snacking options and the growing popularity of plant-based diets. According to industry reports, the global raisin market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, presenting lucrative opportunities for B2B buyers.
